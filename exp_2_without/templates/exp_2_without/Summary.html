A visual summary of the steps of this experiment is included below

1. Coffee Sourcing Stage

● You arrive
● You will be seated
● You will do two pre-experiment games
● You will have three chances to make the supplier a wholesale price offer (W) for one pound your selected coffee
● You will learn if your offer is accepted

2. Coffee Selling Stage

● Your coffee quality assessment will be shared with you to assist you in setting a retail price
● You will set the retail price (R) for one 8 oz cup of brewed coffee made with your selected bean
● You will see the results of your retail price choice, and learn your final payment
